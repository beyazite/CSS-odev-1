Alberto Forpe was born in Tusco, Italy in 1958 on a bright night full of with stars, which later interpreted as a sign of his career success by his aunt Jennie.
He started his career at the age of 16 with his high school band as lead vocalist.
His next jump occured when he was in college and gained more popularity by singing in local bars and then his first album contract with Pupu Records in 1978.
In 1979, his debut album "The Core" was published and next year he had his first country wide tour "The Core Case".
Until his last album "Serenity" in 2013, he released 4 other solo albums.
